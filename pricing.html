Pricing FAQ

Is this really a one-time payment?

Yes! You pay once and get a lifetime license for the major version you purchase (e.g., version 2.x). All updates within that version are free. We don't believe in charging monthly fees for software that runs on your own computer.

Can I use it on multiple computers?

The Personal License is for use on one computer only. If you need to use PDF Fusion on multiple computers, you'll need to purchase additional licenses or consider our Business License which covers up to 5 computers.

What happens after version 2.x?

When we release version 3.0 (which won't be for several years), you'll have the option to upgrade at a discounted price. However, version 2.x will continue to work perfectly, and you'll receive all security and bug fix updates for it indefinitely.

Do you offer educational or nonprofit discounts?

Yes! We offer a 40% discount for students, teachers, and verified nonprofit organizations. Please contact our sales team with proof of your status to receive a discount code.
