Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about PDF Fusion

Installation & Setup

How do I install PDF Fusion?

1. Download the installer from our Download page
2. Run the installer file (PDF-Fusion-Setup.exe for Windows, PDF-Fusion.dmg for Mac)
3. Follow the on-screen instructions
4. Launch PDF Fusion and start your 7-day free trial

What are the system requirements?

Windows: Windows 10 or 11 (64-bit), 4GB RAM minimum (8GB recommended), 500MB free disk space, .NET Framework 4.8 or later.
macOS: macOS 10.15 (Catalina) or later, 4GB RAM minimum, 500MB free disk space.

How do I change the language?

Go to Settings > Language and select from 20+ available languages. The change takes effect immediately.

Features & Usage

Does PDF Fusion work offline?

Yes, 100% offline! PDF Fusion is a desktop application that processes all files locally on your computer. Your documents never leave your machine, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

How many files can I process at once?

PDF Fusion has no hard limits on batch processing. You can process hundreds or even thousands of files simultaneously, limited only by your computer's memory and processing power.

Can I convert scanned PDFs to Word?

Yes, PDF Fusion includes OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology that can convert scanned PDFs into editable Word documents. The accuracy depends on the scan quality, but it's highly effective for most documents.
