Frequently Asked Questions

Is PDF Fusion Pro free?

PDF Fusion Pro offers a 7-day full-feature free trial. After the trial period, you'll need to purchase a license for $69 (one-time payment) to continue using all features.

What systems are supported?

PDF Fusion Pro currently supports Windows 10 and Windows 11 (64-bit). We're working on macOS and Linux versions, which will be available soon.

Is there an online version?

No, PDF Fusion Pro is a desktop application that works 100% offline. This ensures maximum security and privacy for your documents, as they never leave your computer.

How does payment work?

We offer multiple payment options for your convenience:

  • Gumroad (credit cards) - our preferred option for its security and reliability
  • PayPal - for users who prefer PayPal's payment system
  • Bank transfer - available for enterprise customers
Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with PDF Fusion Pro for any reason, simply contact us within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.

How do I update PDF Fusion Pro?

PDF Fusion Pro automatically checks for updates when you launch the software. You can also manually check for updates by going to the "Help" menu and selecting "Check for Updates". Updates are free for all users with a valid license.

How many devices can I use my license on?

Each PDF Fusion Pro license is valid for use on one device. If you need to use the software on multiple devices, please purchase additional licenses.

Does PDF Fusion Pro support batch processing?

Yes! PDF Fusion Pro includes powerful batch processing capabilities that allow you to convert, watermark, compress, and perform other operations on hundreds of files at once.
